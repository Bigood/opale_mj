Who is Opale Starter for?

For trainers and teachers wishing to easily create training modules on web and paper medium.

Why use Opale Starter?

To...

  • Produce accessible online training modules (html) and print resources;

  • Facilitate content updates thanks to single sourcing mechanisms: a single source of content, multiple output media;

  • Improve your content by adding multimedia resources (videos, animations, sounds, images, diagrams, mathematical formulae,...);

  • Improve your content by adding quizzes automatically managed by Opale Starter (Single Choice Questions and Multiple Choice Questions).

And also to...

  • Bring a homogenous graphic identity to all your training documents;

  • Ensure content durability by saving it in an XML format that does not depend on the publishing tool.

OpenDocument publishing

If a problem occurs when publishing paper documents or web documents with OpenDocument tables or graphs, check that you installed precisely the recommended version of LibreOffice (4.2.6), and you might also need to configure it's path in some case, with the menu: Application / Preferences / General / External tools.
